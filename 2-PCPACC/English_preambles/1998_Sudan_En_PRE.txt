THE CONSTITUTION OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE SUDAN 1998 In the name of God, the creator of man and people, the grantor of life and freedom, and the guiding legislator of all society. We, the people of Sudan, with the help of God, cognisant of the lessons of history, and with the help of the revolution of National Salvation, have made this Constitution to establish a public order, which we undertake to respect and protect, so help us God.
